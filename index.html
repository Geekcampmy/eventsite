About Us

We're a group of techies of various backgrounds and interests, in the field of hardware, software, enterprise, mobile and startups. Our common interests revolve around providing the community a platform to meet, share and learn from each other. 

What is Our Goal?

This conference aims to provide a platform for sharing and networking. Topics must be technical but can be in one of many fields:

  • Internet of Things (IoT) / Hardware
  • Web / Mobile
  • Programming languages / frameworks / concepts
  • DevOps / Automation / SaaS / PaaS / IaaS
  • Scaling Applications / Architecture
  • Software Engineering & Organizational Practices
  • Big Data / Analytics
  • What ever is cutting edge
